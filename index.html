Mehmān-navāzi — the art of the welcome

In Iran, the guest comes first

That old Persian idea sits at the centre of every plate we serve. Our kitchen works buffet-style, so you build the plate you feel like today — a little of this, more of that, always fresh. We slow-cook, char-grill and layer saffron and dried lime the way it's done at home, then let you choose your sides.

  • Char-grilled kebabs
  • Saffron rice
